Smart Homes Electrical Company Limited acronym (SHECL) I duly incorporated in Trinidad and Tobago. Electricity is an essential service that is always needed in this power-hungry world. SHECL's main services are General Electrical works (domestic and industrial) with the installation of high-end electrical systems for security, data transfer and communication. In terms of sustainability and diversification SHECL is exploring opportunities to integrate Smart Home Technology and Climate Change Solutions to our portfolio. (SHECL), provides the satisfaction of human wants through sustainable smart home systems while still providing traditional wiring to customers. Lets be honest electrical installation is changing, lower power factor bigger switch gear and cables fighting with power management now the industry has power management on a different level by using extra low voltage systems, mini-computers and different features to manage safe cost and reduce the load taken from supply grid. After years of traditional wiring in Trinidad and Tobago we have seen a need for upgrading electrical systems for power management, lighting and security features. SHECL has that vision for smart housing in this beautiful country, we have tools, knowledge and opportunity where we can accomplish housing for security, lighting, electronic locking, occupancy senser, camera systems and so much more for persons where they can feel at rest when leaving there homes, when they are not, its been growing and new for the past few years companies like SHECL have all moved into this market of the new age wiring system.
